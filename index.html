Our Story

A pioneer in Montessori
education since 1999.

Little Hearts Kindergarten has been shaping futures in South B for 25 years — the first school in the area to introduce Montessori learning, and still the best.

Our Journey

25 years of nurturing
South B's children.

What started in 1999 as a small kindergarten with a bold idea — that Montessori learning could transform early childhood education in South B — has become a beloved institution serving hundreds of Nairobi families.

1999
Founded — a pioneer is born
Little Hearts opens its doors on Daidai Road, South B — the first kindergarten in the area to introduce authentic Montessori education.
2005
Computer Lab launched
One of the first kindergartens in South B to introduce a dedicated computer laboratory for early technology literacy.
2017
CBC curriculum adopted
Successfully integrated Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) with our existing Montessori framework — combining the best of both worlds.
2024
25th anniversary celebrations
A landmark year — celebrating a quarter century of shaping curious, confident, and capable young minds across Nairobi.
Our Philosophy

Everything we do starts
with one belief: every child is special.

Montessori at heart
Child-led learning

In the Montessori classroom, the teacher is not at the front of the room. She moves throughout, giving lessons to individual children. Every child works at their own pace on materials designed to build specific skills.

CBC compliant
Future-ready curriculum

We follow the Ministry of Education's CBC framework as specified in the Basic Education Curriculum Framework (BECF), enhanced with Montessori methodology — so our children are both independent thinkers and prepared for formal school.

Small class policy
Maximum 15 learners

We cap every class at 15 children — not because we have to, but because we believe real learning happens when a teacher can truly see every child. Individual attention is not a luxury here; it is the standard.

Community first
All backgrounds welcome

Little Hearts is run on Islamic values and admits children of all races, religions, and ethnicities without any discrimination. We are a school where every family feels respected, welcomed, and valued as an active partner.

Our Curriculum

CBC meets Montessori.
The best of both.

We follow Kenya's Ministry of Education CBC framework — enhanced with 25 years of Montessori expertise. Children graduate ready for formal school, genuinely curious, and confident.

Classes Offered

We welcome children
from ages 2 to 6.

Ages 2–3
Playgroup
Max 15 children

Toilet training, social skills, and structured play. Staff helpers attend to every need. A gentle, safe first school experience.

Ages 3–4
Pre-Nursery
Max 15 children

Language development, early numeracy, and expressive arts. Building foundational skills through play and guided Montessori activities.

Ages 4–5
PP1
Max 15 children

Reading readiness, number work, science exploration, and creative expression. The Montessori materials make abstract concepts tangible and exciting.

Ages 5–6
PP2
Max 15 children

Full CBC PP2 syllabus preparation for Grade 1. Reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies. Children leave confident and school-ready.

Our Approach

Learning that lasts
a lifetime.

Little Hearts was the first kindergarten in South B to introduce Montessori — and we've spent 25 years perfecting how it blends with Kenya's CBC. The result: children who love learning, focus well, and develop the collaborative skills that prestigious schools and careers demand.

CBC Compliant Authentic Montessori Holistic Development
📖Literacy — phonics, reading readiness, and storytelling from Playgroup onwards
🔢Numeracy — Montessori bead chains, number rods, and practical maths activities
🌿Environmental activities — nature walks, science experiments, and observation journals
🎨Creative arts — drawing, painting, craft, and music integrated into daily learning
🙏Values education — respect, responsibility, and empathy taught by example daily
💻Technology — weekly computer lab sessions with age-appropriate learning software
Co-Curricular Activities

Beyond the classroom —
building the whole child.

Professional trainers and qualified staff run a range of co-curricular activities designed to build confidence, fitness, and skills beyond the classroom.

Weekly · Qualified Instructors
Swimming

Children attend Bellevue School's swimming pool once a week, trained by qualified instructors. Water safety and swimming confidence built from an early age.

Weekly · Computer Lab
Technology & Computing

Modern computer laboratory with the latest learning software. An expert trainer delivers structured lessons weekly with a full syllabus.

Weekly · Well-stocked
Library & E-Learning

A big, beautiful library visited weekly. Older children take a book home every week. E-learning sessions and reading programmes also run here.

Daily · All Classes
Arts & Craft

Daily creative expression through art, craft, and drawing. All stationery — books, pencils, paint, and crayons — is provided by the school.

Daily · Turf Playground
Outdoor Play

A beautiful, well-equipped playground with a turf floor for safe play. Children have daily outdoor time to develop physical skills and social bonds.

Daily · No Extra Cost
Madrassah Classes

A qualified Madrassah teacher teaches Muslim children daily at no extra charge. Progress reports issued every end of term so parents stay informed.

Our Facilities

Every facility built
for young learners.

From the turf-padded playground to the well-stocked library and modern computer lab — every space at Little Hearts is designed to make your child's day safe, stimulating, and joyful.

What We Have

Everything your child
needs, all in one place.

🌿
Turf Playground

A beautiful, well-equipped playground with a padded turf floor — giving children a safe surface to run, climb, and play freely every single day during break time.

💻
Computer Lab

Modern, well-equipped computer laboratory with the latest age-appropriate learning software. An expert trainer delivers weekly structured lessons with a clear syllabus.

📚
Library & E-Learning Centre

A big, beautifully-stocked library used weekly by every class. Older children take a book home each week. E-learning sessions also take place in this space.

🍽️
On-Site Kitchen & Dining

All meals are cooked fresh in school daily using quality ingredients. The full lunch menu includes a main dish plus dessert. A snack and milk are served at 10AM — all included.

🛁
Child-Friendly Washrooms

Safe, child-sized bathroom facilities maintained to the highest standard of hygiene. Staff helpers assist younger children with toilet training and handwashing.

😴
Nap Room

A dedicated, quiet rest space for full-day children after lunch. Short supervised nap keeps energy levels fresh and positive for the afternoon session.

🕌
Madrassah Classroom

A quiet, dedicated space for daily Madrassah lessons taught by a qualified Islamic teacher — available at no extra cost to Muslim children every school day.

🎨
Art & Craft Studio

A colourful, well-supplied creative space for daily arts and crafts. All stationery, paint, crayons, and drawing materials are provided by the school — nothing extra needed.

🚌
Transport Service

Door-to-door morning pick-up and afternoon/lunch drop-off. A personalised, supervised service ensuring every child is collected by and handed to a responsible adult.

Meals Programme

Nutritious food,
cooked with care.

You never need to send your child to school with food. Everything is provided — prepared fresh in school every day using the best ingredients to keep children happy, healthy, and energised for learning.

Daily Meal Schedule
Morning snack + milk10:00 AM
Hot lunch (main dish + dessert)12:30 PM
Afternoon snack (full-day)2:30 PM

A detailed break menu is available for parents to view during school visits. All meals are prepared with care and nutritional balance in mind — no junk, no shortcuts.

Transport Service

Safe, personalised
door-to-door transport.

Our transport service is designed around the working parent. We pick your child from your door each morning and drop them home safely at lunch or afternoon — every time, to a responsible adult.

🏠
Door-to-door pick-up

We collect from your home address every school morning — no need to arrange a drop-off point or alter your commute.

🔒
Supervised handover, always

Every child is personally handed to a known, responsible adult at drop time — never left unattended or with an unknown person.

⏰
Lunch or afternoon drop options

Choose between lunch-time (1PM) or afternoon (3:30PM) drop-off depending on your family schedule and chosen programme.

Admissions Open

journey with us.

We welcome children aged 2–6. Admissions are ongoing throughout the year. Book a school tour, meet the teachers, and see Little Hearts for yourself.

Admission Process

Simple, welcoming,
and straightforward.

1
Book a tour

Call or WhatsApp us to schedule a visit. Meet the teachers, see the classrooms, and let your child explore the playground.

2
Collect forms

Download or collect an admission form from school. Fill in your child's details and return with the required documents.

3
Pay and confirm

Settle the registration and first-term fees. We confirm your child's class placement and start date in writing.

4
First day!

Your child joins the Little Hearts family. Our team ensures every new child settles in with warmth and confidence from day one.
